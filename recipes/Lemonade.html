Lemonade

Description

This lemonade is good on hot days, and very successful at lemonade stands.

Ingredients

  • Lemons, juiced
  • Water
  • Brown sugar
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. In a large pitcher, combine fresh lemon juice and water.
  2. Stir in brown sugar until it dissolves.
  3. Stir in ice cubes.
